Registration Process

A simple 4-step path to join Zero Gravity.

This is the full flow every applicant follows before appearing in the community.

1

Enter with Google

Start from the login page and sign in with your Google account before opening the site.

2

Prepare your profile

Keep your college, role, skills, project links, and portfolio details ready before you fill the form.

3

Submit the application

Tell us what you have built, how you want to contribute, and why you want to join the community.

4

Wait for admin review

Approved applicants are added to the visible community section after review by the team.

Before You Apply

What to prepare for a strong application.

Clear and honest details help us review faster. If you have built real things, this is the place to show them.

🧾
Basic details

Full name, phone number, college, department, and year of study.

🛠️
Skills and role

Your preferred role, area of interest, tools, and practical strengths.

🔗
Proof of work

LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio links, or any work that shows what you can really do.

🚀
Your intent

Explain what you want to contribute and why Zero Gravity is the right place for you.

Tip: the strongest applications are specific, practical, and backed by real projects or experiments.
