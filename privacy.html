Policy

Privacy Policy

We collect only the details needed to respond to enquiries, review member applications, and improve the website experience.

What We Collect

When you submit a client enquiry or a member application, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, college or organization, project details, skills, portfolio links, and other information you voluntarily share with us.

How We Use It

  • To respond to project enquiries and problem statements.
  • To review students who want to join or work with us.
  • To maintain approved member information shown publicly on the website.
  • To understand website usage, including visitor count analytics.

Data Storage

Form submissions and approved member records are stored in our Supabase database. We do not sell or trade your information. We only use it for communication, team review, and internal coordination.

Cookies and Local Storage

The website may use browser storage for sign-in state, visitor counting, and smoother form experiences. This helps us avoid duplicate actions and improve usability.

Your Rights

If you want your submitted details corrected or removed, contact us at teamzerogravityorg@gmail.com. We will review the request and respond as soon as possible.
