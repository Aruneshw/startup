Our Story

Team Zero Gravity started in April 2026 at Bannari Amman Institute of Technology (BIT Sathy), Tamil Nadu, when a small group of engineering students decided to learn faster by building together.

We quickly grew into a cross-disciplinary team spanning AIML, ECE, EEE, and IT, united by a shared interest in intelligent systems, robotics, cybersecurity, and practical impact.

From phishing detection and RoboSoccer to SurgiScore and PulseNet+, we use every event and build to test ideas and ship stronger systems.

“We don't prototype for the shelf. We build for the real world.”

— Team Zero Gravity
