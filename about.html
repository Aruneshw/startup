How It Started

Zero Gravity began in April 2026 at Bannari Amman Institute of Technology (BIT Sathy), Tamil Nadu, when a small group of engineering students decided to learn faster by building together.

We quickly realized that many startups, small businesses, and individuals face the same problem: finding reliable, affordable technical support for solving real-world challenges, bugs, coding issues, and full project builds.

At the same time, skilled college students like us lacked access to real-world opportunities to apply our knowledge, build credibility, and earn income. Existing platforms either focused only on freelancing or only on discussion-based solutions. None combined real problem-solving, trust, and skill validation in one path.

That is why we built Zero Gravity: a tech ecosystem that connects clients with college students and developers to solve real-world technical problems through a competitive, community-driven model.

"We do not just solve problems. We build a place where solving problems is how you learn, earn, and prove your skills."

— Zero Gravity
